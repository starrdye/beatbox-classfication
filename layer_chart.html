🎵
Input — Log Mel-Spectrogram
Shape: (1 × 64 × 128)
Single-channel 2D time-frequency image. 64 Mel bins × 128 time frames ≈ 3 s of audio at sr=22,050 Hz.
🔲
Conv Block 1
Conv2D(1→16, 3×3) → BatchNorm → ReLU → MaxPool(2×2)
Applies 16 learnable 3×3 filters. MaxPool halves frequency and time dimensions. Output: (16 × 32 × 64).
🔲
Conv Block 2
Conv2D(16→32, 3×3) → BatchNorm → ReLU → MaxPool(2×2)
Doubles the feature maps to 32. Learns combinations of frequency and time patterns from Block 1. Output: (32 × 16 × 32).
🔲
Conv Block 3
Conv2D(32→64, 3×3) → BatchNorm → ReLU → AdaptiveAvgPool(4×4)
64 feature maps, pooled to fixed 4×4 grid regardless of input size. Output: (64 × 4 × 4) = 1,024 values.
⠿
Classifier Head
Flatten → Linear(1024→128) → ReLU → Dropout(0.5)
Flattens 1,024 values, projects to 128-dim representation. Dropout randomly zeroes 50% of neurons per training step to prevent memorisation.
✓
Output — Softmax (4 classes)
Linear(128→4) → Softmax
Converts 4 raw scores to a probability distribution summing to 1. Predicted class = highest probability.

Bright vertical stripe concentrated in low Mel bins. Fast onset, fast decay.
k — K-Snare
Broadband vertical transient across all frequencies. Near-instantaneous.
nu — Hum
Stable, narrow horizontal band. Low ZCR, stable MFCCs over time.

Sustained high-frequency noise band. Gradual decay, high ZCR throughout.
